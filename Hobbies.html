Description of VideoGames

Call of Duty Black Ops 3: Call of Duty Black Ops 3 or Cod BO3 is a first person shooter where you can boost jump around the map and kill other players. Or you can play the campaign where you do story mode. Or the final mode is zombies where you fight zombies with other people.
Minecraft: Minecraft is a game where you spawn into a world with nothing and can mine trees and get materials and do whatever you want. Or you can join servers where people modded the game and added in all sorts of mini games.
State of Decay 2: State of Decay 2 is a zombie survival game where you have to make a base and escape zombies. You can drive aroud the map and switch to different charecters to fight zombies or humans or make friends.
