Games

I play lots of games with my friends like Ghost in the Graveyard and Infected. Ghost in the Graveyard is a game where one person is the ghost and they go to hide in the backyard and hide. Then everyone else counts to 30 or whatever you want to count to. After that you yell that you are coming and you have to go all the way around the house with out getting tagged. If the ghost tags you then you are the ghost next round. Infected is where one person is the first infected and they have to count to 20 then they try to tag people and if you get tagged then you are also infected. Last one suviving wins.

More Games

Another game we like to play and probaly our favorite is called Flying Cows. We dont know why it is called this but it just is. First you need to find a playground where it is not to big or just make boundries. Then you need 3 people and one person is tagger. The tagger goes to bottom of stairs, ladder or whereever you want them to start. Then they close their eyes and count to 20. They then have to close their eyes and go around the playground and hunt people down. You are not allowed to touch the ground or leave the playground. So when the taggers tag someone the person they tag are it.
